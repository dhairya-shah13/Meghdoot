Our Legacy

Over Two Decades of Automobile Excellence

Founded in June 1999 as Mini Motors, the workshop began with a singular vision: to redefine the service experience for vehicle owners through transparency and technical mastery. Our journey reached a pivotal milestone on February 22, 2002, when we were officially appointed as a Maruti Suzuki Authorized Service Station and rebranded as Meghdoot Motors.

Led by visionaries Niraj Chokshi and Rakesh Gajjar, we have grown from a local workshop into a state-of-the-art service hub, maintaining the highest standards of Maruti Suzuki's global service protocols.
